Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026 · Last updated: June 27, 2026

Zentryx Interactive LLC ("Zentryx Interactive," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Furavis AI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect basic information necessary to identify you and provide the service:

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Authentication credentials managed securely through Firebase Authentication

Pet Information

To provide personalized health tracking, we collect information you provide about your pets, including:

  • Pet name, species, breed, age, and weight
  • Vaccination records and medical history you choose to enter
  • Profile photos you upload (optional)
  • Notes and observations you log about your pet

Health Data

The core function of Furavis AI is health monitoring. When you use health-tracking features, we collect:

  • Symptoms and health observations you describe or report
  • Health check entries, logs, and trends over time
  • Responses and assessments generated through AI analysis of your inputs
  • Location data for nearby veterinary clinic searches (only when you initiate a search)

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain technical information to maintain and improve the App:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Feature usage patterns and session duration (anonymized)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs
  • Subscription status and purchase history (processed by RevenueCat)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

AI-Powered Health Analysis

Symptom descriptions and pet health information you provide are sent to the Anthropic Claude API to generate health assessments, suggestions, and educational content. This is the primary function of the App. Inputs are transmitted securely and are not used by Anthropic to train their models.

Health Tracking and History

Your pet's health logs, check-ins, and recorded observations are stored in your account to provide trend tracking, historical context, and continuity between sessions.

App Functionality and Account Management

We use your account information to authenticate you, maintain your preferences, sync data across devices, and deliver subscription features you have purchased.

Service Improvement

Anonymized usage data helps us identify bugs, improve features, and understand how users interact with the App. We do not sell or share this data with advertisers.

Communications

We may use your email address to send transactional messages such as account confirmations, subscription receipts, and important policy updates. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit opt-in.

3. Third-Party Services

Furavis AI integrates the following third-party services to deliver its functionality. Each service operates under its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase for user authentication, cloud data storage (Firestore), and crash reporting (Crashlytics). Your account data and pet records are stored in Firebase under Google's infrastructure. Google's Privacy Policy applies to Firebase services.

RevenueCat

Subscription purchases and entitlement management are handled by RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives your anonymized user ID and purchase transaction data from Google Play or the App Store to verify and manage your subscription status. RevenueCat's Privacy Policy governs the handling of this data.

Anthropic Claude API

AI-generated health assessments are powered by Anthropic's Claude API. When you submit a health query, your input (along with relevant pet context) is transmitted to Anthropic's servers to generate a response. Anthropic's API usage policies apply. Inputs are processed to generate your response and are not used by Anthropic to train future models under their standard API terms.

Google Places API

When you use the nearby veterinary clinic search feature, we query the Google Places API using your device's approximate location. This location data is sent directly to Google to return relevant results and is not stored by us beyond the duration of your search session. Google's Privacy Policy applies to this data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third parties for marketing purposes. Data is shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With the third-party service providers listed above, solely to deliver the App's features
  • When required by law, court order, or valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Zentryx Interactive, our users, or the public
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, in which case users will be notified

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data and pet health records for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your personal data and associated pet records are permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Anonymized usage and diagnostic data may be retained in aggregate form beyond account deletion, as it no longer identifies you personally.

Subscription transaction records are retained for the period required by applicable tax and financial regulations, typically seven years.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access. You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction. You may update or correct inaccurate information through the App settings or by contacting us.
  • Deletion. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by contacting us or using the account deletion option within the App.
  • Portability. You may request an export of your pet health data in a common format.
  • Objection. You may object to certain types of processing, such as analytics data collection.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@zentryxinteractive.io. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Age Requirements

Furavis AI is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at support@zentryxinteractive.io and we will delete that information promptly.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure cloud storage through Firebase, and access controls that limit who within our organization can access user data. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App, applicable law, or our data practices. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via email or an in-app notice. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
